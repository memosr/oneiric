This dream reflects a liminal condition within the individuation           
     process; the stopped clocks represent the timeless realm of the            
     unconscious, while the suitcase that cannot be abandoned signifies a       
     complex that cannot be surrendered to the unconscious. The man at the      
     ticket counter's statement that every ticket goes to the same place        
     constitutes a message from the wise-trickster archetype, indicating        
     that all paths in life lead toward the Self or the ultimate universal      
     truth. As in the earlier Sacred Thresholds dream, the dreamer once         
     again stands within the transitional space between two worlds; the         
     endless corridor signifies the boundless journey into the depths of        
     the psyche and the never-ending process of consciousness expansion.
