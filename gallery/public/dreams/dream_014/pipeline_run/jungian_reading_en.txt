This dream demonstrates how exceedingly permeable the membrane between     
     consciousness and the unconscious truly is — walls trembling like          
     gauze, your fingers passing through to the other side upon contact,        
     the realization dawning that the boundary is illusory. The handleless      
     door represents a psychic state that resists forcible opening; yet the     
     discovery that the door has, in fact, been open all along reveals that     
     the Self is perpetually accessible, though such accessibility requires     
     the act of recognition. As with the thresholds in your previous dreams     
     (the station, the infinite corridor), the Trickster archetype is           
     operative here as well — in a realm where the ostensibly closed is         
     open, where the interior is exterior, you forfeit your sense of            
     orientation. Your inability to determine which side you have crossed       
     indicates progression toward the unconscious within the indeterminacy      
     that characterizes the path toward wholeness.
