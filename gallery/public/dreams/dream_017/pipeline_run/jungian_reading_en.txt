This dream reveals the individuation journey unfolding within the          
     depths of the unconscious. The luminous leaves signify potentials          
     existent yet unrecognized by consciousness; the reflection upon the        
     stream's surface constitutes the manifestation of the Shadow               
     archetype. The motif of the Waiting Shadow from previous dreams            
     reemerges here—the dream terminates once more at the moment of             
     encounter, indicating an integration process as yet incomplete. The        
     unseen face represents a portion of self-knowledge for which the           
     subject is not yet prepared; that distance of several steps ahead          
     embodies the concretization of tension between consciousness and the       
     unconscious.
