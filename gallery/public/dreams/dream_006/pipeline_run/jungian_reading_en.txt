This dream constitutes a profound reflection of the individuation          
     process. The shimmering stones beneath the feet signify the perpetual      
     rebirth of consciousness with each step undertaken; the                    
     downward-gazing tree, meanwhile, serves as a symbol of the introverted     
     quest and the roots extending toward the unconscious. The gate             
     composed of light droplets functions as a liminal threshold opening        
     onto the collective unconscious—the arrival not of wind, but rather of     
     "the voice of another time," indicates that the individual has             
     achieved access to the experience of timelessness and archetypal           
     reality. The silent creatures dwelling beneath the clouds represent        
     the integration of the Shadow, encountered not with trepidation but        
     with serenity.
