This dream symbolizes the encounter with the unconscious during the        
     individuation process. The solitary tree represents Jung's concept of      
     the Self—the symbol of wholeness and the inner center. The shadow that     
     does not rest upon the ground indicates the elevation of the shadow        
     archetype to the level of consciousness, signifying that the time has      
     come to confront one's repressed aspects. The cessation of wind upon       
     touching the shadow constitutes a sacred moment in which the veil          
     between consciousness and the unconscious is lifted, and                   
     transformation is actualized.
