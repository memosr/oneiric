This dream constitutes a symbol of the profound bond established with      
     the unconscious. The forest represents the domain of the collective        
     unconscious and the as-yet-undiscovered potentials of the self. The        
     lights within the water signify the luminous aspects of the anima—the      
     soul—serving as indications of sacred content emerging from the depths     
     of the unconscious toward the surface. The illumination of the forest      
     upon contact with the water demonstrates that this engagement with the     
     unconscious enlightens the individual and indicates the experience of      
     a significant moment within the individuation process.
