I’m walking down a quiet street… yellow lamps reflecting on the wet stones, a faint clock tower in the distance.

I pass by the bakery. The door is slightly open, and the warm steam inside drifts out, turning into tiny glowing lights. I follow them.

The road opens into a field. The wind moves through the grass. In the distance, a single tree… its shadow hanging in the air.

Suddenly, I’m at the shore. A small boat rests by the water. I look into the surface…

It’s not just me in the reflection.

The street, the bakery, the field, and that shadow… all staring back with me.
