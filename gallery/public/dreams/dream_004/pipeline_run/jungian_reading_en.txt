This dream constitutes a profound reflection of the individuation          
     process. While the misty city represents the conscious mind, the           
     golden light symbolizes the illumination of the Self. The                  
     transformation of leaves into fireflies indicates a spiritual              
     metamorphosis and an alchemical operation. The lake, reflecting            
     galaxies in place of the sky, serves as a window into the depths of        
     the collective unconscious. The silhouette at the water's edge             
     represents the encounter between the conscious ego and the unconscious     
     cosmos. The obscured face signifies that this encounter remains an         
     incomplete, intrapsychic process; the ripples generated by the droplet     
     indicate the activation of latent potentials within the unconscious.
