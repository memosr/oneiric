About

Oneiric turns text-described dreams into Salvador Dalí-style cards. Every card contains three AI-generated scenes, a Jungian reading, and a symbolic analysis — all produced by a pipeline running entirely on Nous Research's Hermes Agent with Kimi K2.5, FAL image generation, and OpenAI TTS.

Built for the Nous Research Creative Hackathon 2026.
